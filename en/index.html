About the Historic Center

Capital of Paraíba State, João Pessoa has one of the oldest historical centers in the country, full of churches, monuments, historic squares, obelisks and fountains.

Places of interest

Twenty-five places of interest were selected, 11 associated with the cultural environment and 14 with the abiotic environment.

How to get here

Located at the eastern end of the Americas, João Pessoa is easily accessible via Castro Pinto Airport, 10 km away and Severino Camelo Bus Terminal, in downtown João Pessoa.
